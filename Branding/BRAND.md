# Glow — Brand Guide

**Glow** is a private, on-device iOS + Apple Watch app for personal fitness,
skincare, and nutrition routines. Tagline: *Show up. Glow up.*

## Logo

A white **flame** glyph on the warm coral→amber gradient. The flame represents
the daily streak — consistency is the core habit Glow rewards.

- `Branding/glow-icon-512.png` — master mark
- `Glow/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/icon-1024.png` — App Store / TestFlight icon

## Color

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Coral | `#FF7E5F` | Primary accent (gradient start) |
| Amber | `#FEB47B` | Accent gradient end |
| Ink | `#121212` / `#F5F5F5` | Primary text (light / dark) |
| Background | `#F2F2F2` / `#1A1A1A` | Page background (light / dark) |
| Surface | `#FFFFFF` / `#292929` | Cards & panels |

The warm coral→amber gradient is the **only** color in the app. Everything else
is monochrome — the design is minimal, type-led, with generous whitespace.

## Typography

System font (SF), heavy weights for big numerals and UPPERCASE section
headings. Big-date numeral header, struck-through completed items in accent.

## Voice

Direct, encouraging, never preachy. Privacy-first: *your data stays on your
device.*

## Layout motifs

- **Day-stack checklist** — today's routines as a tappable checklist.
- **Dot-calendar** — month grid; completed days fill a dot, today is the accent dot.
- **Bento stat grid** — big-number cards (one dark, one accent) for progress.
