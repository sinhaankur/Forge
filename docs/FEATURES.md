# Forge — Features

Forge is a private, on-device iOS + watchOS app for the human basics:
**move, sleep, skin, fuel** — personalized by your biology.

## Today (home)
- Pure-black bento dashboard: personalized greeting, completion ring, streak,
  3-month consistency dots, weekly workouts, daily **readiness** row.
- **Plan-around-your-day** card: reads your calendar, finds free time around
  meetings, and schedules workout/warm-up reminders.

## Fitness
- **Generate a personalized CrossFit plan** from your profile (height, weight,
  build, experience, injuries) — always injury-screened, warm-up first.
- **Guided "Focus" session player** — full-screen, one step at a time, with a
  built-in timer, form cues, and haptics. Press play and follow.
- **How-to guides** for each movement: step-by-step form, target muscles, common
  mistakes, scaling, breathing.
- **Smart add**: a time-of-day + readiness-aware suggestion, or paste a plan from
  any AI (Gemini/ChatGPT) and Forge turns it into an editable routine
  (on-device, via Apple Foundation Models when available).
- **Per-workout targets** (reps/weight/duration/distance/calories) with logging.
- **Feel-good boost** tags: the brain chemistry a session tends to promote
  (endorphins/dopamine/serotonin/BDNF/cortisol-down), tuned by your DNA.

## Sleep & Readiness
- Last night's sleep from Apple Health or a manual log; 7-day trend.
- A transparent **0–100 readiness score** (sleep + recent training load +
  consistency) with training advice and a factor breakdown.
- Evidence-based sleep-hygiene tips.

## Skincare
- Morning & evening routines with reminders.

## Nutrition
- A high-protein, body-recomposition meal plan with a daily protein tracker.
- **Genetic fueling** card: carb timing, dairy/whey, vitamin D & B12 guidance
  driven by your on-device DNA traits.

## DNA Insights
- Import a raw DNA file (23andMe / AncestryDNA / MyHeritage) — parsed
  **on-device** — to derive coarse trait categories (never raw genotypes).
- Visualize as **trait cards / radar chart / genome map**, each explaining the
  gene, SNP, and how your genotype maps to the result, plus what lifestyle can
  change.

## Progress
- Streaks, dot-calendar consistency, bento stat cards.
- **Activity patterns** (driving/walking/running/cycling/sitting) from
  CoreMotion, plus steps/distance (CoreMotion or Apple Health).

## Apple Watch
- Glanceable today's routines, check-offs, and streak — synced from the phone.
  See [WATCH_SYNC.md](WATCH_SYNC.md).

## Personal & private
- On-device profile (name + photo), no account, no server, no analytics.
- See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) and [ARCHITECTURE.md](ARCHITECTURE.md).
